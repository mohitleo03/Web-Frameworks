Whats it like?

We are focused on attracting, retaining and engaging the best talent in our markets so our people remain empowered and inspired by one another. In GDS, you will collaborate with EY teams on exciting projects and work with well-known brands from across the globe. Well introduce you to an ever-expanding ecosystem of people, learning, skills and insights that will stay with you throughout your career.




Join one of our dynamic teams

From accountants to coders, we offer a wide variety of fulfilling career opportunities that span all business disciplines. We look for skills that are evergreen and our roles evolve with industry trends. Some of our GDS roles include:



  • Accountants
  • Cyber Security Specialists and Project Managers
  • Business Tax Specialists
  • M&A Consultants and Finance Managers

We also work across Finance, Business Development, Technology, Talent, Procurement and Risk Management functions to help EY teams operate as efficiently and effectively as possible.






Varied and vital
